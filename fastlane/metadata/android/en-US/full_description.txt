T_Launcher is a sophisticated Android home screen designed to eliminate distractions and force productivity.

KEY FEATURES:

• Dynamic Motivational Engine
Every day generates a new high-contrast wallpaper with unique geometric patterns (Bauhaus, Cyber, Hexagon) and motivational quotes to keep you focused.

• Developer Panel
Built-in LeetCode dashboard featuring:
  - Daily coding challenges
  - Activity heatmap for consistency tracking
  - Friends leaderboard

• Focus Mode
Strictly enforced distraction-free environment:
  - Immersive isolation with solid themes
  - 15-minute cooldown between pauses
  - App whitelisting
  - Authentication-required exit

• Distraction-Free Interface
No icons on home screen. Only time, date, and your mission.

• Privacy-Focused
Zero ads, zero trackers, compatible with work profiles.

• Comprehensive Gestures
Supports swipes, edge gestures, volume buttons, and custom drawings.

CUSTOMIZATION:

• Multiple wallpaper patterns
• Advanced color theory palettes
• 50+ motivational quotes
• Gesture-based app launching
• Customizable shortcuts and actions

TECHNICAL:

• Lightweight (~20 MB)
• Open source (MIT License)
• Kotlin + Jetpack Compose
• No special permissions required
• Works on Android 6.0+
